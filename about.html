ABOUT FUR-EVER BAHAY

Our primary mission at FUR-EVER BAHAY is to educate individuals and communities about the importance of animal welfare, responsible pet ownership, and proper animal care.

We strive to empower people with the knowledge they need to ensure that animals, both domestic and wild, are treated with respect and compassion. In addition to education, we also manage animal control issues, helping to resolve situations where animals may be at risk or pose a danger to themselves or others.

We believe that every animal deserves a life free from suffering and neglect. That's why we provide comprehensive information on a wide range of animal-related topics, from the basics of pet care to more complex issues such as animal behavior, health, and nutrition.

OUR MISSION

The FUR-EVER BAHAY aims to protect animals from cruelty, ease their pain, fear, and suffering, and promote a compassionate society where animals are treated with kindness and respect. They work to raise awareness, educate the public, and encourage responsible pet ownership, making sure that animals are cared for and valued.

OUR VISION

To create a compassionate and cruelty-free world where all animals are treated with respect, kindness, and dignity, living in harmony with humans who embrace humane principles and responsible care for every living being.

OUR VALUES

1.Compassion: Treat all animals with kindness and empathy.

2.Respect: Every animal deserves care and dignity.

3.Responsibility: Promote responsible pet ownership and care.

4.Education: Raise awareness about humane treatment and animal welfare.

5.Collaboration: Work with others to end cruelty and promote animal welfare.
