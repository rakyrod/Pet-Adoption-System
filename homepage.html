Adopt a Shelter Cat and Dog

All of our adoptable cats and dogs have been spayed/neutered and vaccinated. Since they had tough lives before being rescued, we want to make sure they go to kind and caring homes where they’ll be safe and well-loved. Here’s how you can apply to adopt one:

Steps to Adopt:

1. Submit an adoption application

2. Attend the Zoom meeting interview

3. Meet our shelter animals in person

4. Visit your chosen pet to confirm your choice

5. Wait for vet clearance and schedule pick-up

6. Pay the adoption fee

P500 Cat / P1000 Dog

7. Take your pet home!

ABOUT US

Our main job is to teach people about animal welfare, how to care for animals, and manage animal control issues. We also provide information on various animal-related topics.

FUR-EVER BAHAY is dedicated to fighting againsft animal cruelty and pet neglect. We oppose activities such as dogfights, horse fights, and using wild animals for entertainment.
